About

Where technical depth meets field experience.

I am an AI engineer and independent digital consultant with a Master’s degree in Information Systems Design, graduated with Distinction from ISC Goma. For over five years, I have deployed AI tools, ERP systems, and data solutions for organisations across the DRC — in mining, fintech, logistics, and humanitarian sectors.

I grew up in eastern DRC, an environment that demanded resourcefulness, adaptability and a practical approach to every challenge. Those formative years shaped the way I think about technology: not as an end in itself, but as a tool that must work reliably under real conditions, for real people.

Today, based in Kinshasa, I work with organisations seeking to deploy AI, automate their operations, or strengthen their data systems. I have trained over 20 professionals in the DRC on AI tools and delivered complete digital transformation projects across sectors.
